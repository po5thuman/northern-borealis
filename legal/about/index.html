About Northern Borealis

Independent, honest guidance to help you find and book the best Northern Lights tours in Europe.

Welcome to Northern Borealis — your trusted companion for chasing the Northern Lights across Europe. We're a small team of aurora obsessives who've spent countless freezing, wonderful nights beneath the dancing green skies of Norway, Sweden and Finland, and we built this site to help fellow travellers experience that same magic.

Our Story

Northern Borealis was born out of frustration — the good kind. On our first aurora trip years ago, we spent hours trawling through confusing tour listings, contradictory advice and glossy marketing that promised the moon (and the lights). We booked partly on luck, and got lucky. But we walked away thinking: surely there's a better way to help people plan this.

Since then we've returned to the Arctic again and again — to Tromsø, Svalbard, the Lofoten Islands, Abisko, Kiruna and Lapland — learning what actually makes a great Northern Lights tour and what to avoid. Northern Borealis is where we share everything we've learned, so your first aurora hunt (or your fifth) goes as smoothly as possible.

What We Do

We research, compare and rank the best Northern Lights tours from Europe's top aurora destinations. For every location, we highlight the tours we genuinely rate most highly, explain who each one suits — photographers, families, adventurers, budget travellers — and give you the practical details you need to book with confidence.

Our destination guides currently cover:

  • ✅ Norway — Tromsø, Svalbard and Svolvær (Lofoten Islands)
  • ✅ Sweden — Abisko National Park and Kiruna
  • ✅ Finland — Lapland, including Rovaniemi and Levi
  • ✅ And more destinations on the way

How We Choose the Tours

We don't just list every tour we can find. We assess each one against the things that genuinely matter for a great aurora experience:

  • ⭐ Real traveller ratings & review counts — what actual customers experienced.
  • 👥 Group size — smaller groups usually mean a better, more personal night.
  • ✅ What's included — guides, photos, warm clothing, refreshments and transfers.
  • 🛡️ Guarantees — money-back or return policies if the aurora doesn't show.
  • 💶 Overall value — not just the cheapest, but the best experience for the price.

Then we pick the tours we'd happily send our own friends and family on. If we wouldn't book it ourselves, we won't recommend it to you.

Our Promise to You

Honesty first. Our recommendations are independent and never influenced by commissions. We recommend tours on merit alone. (For full transparency on how we're funded, see our Affiliate Disclosure.)

Clear, no-hype information. The aurora is magical enough — it doesn't need exaggeration. We give you straight, practical advice so you know exactly what to expect.

We're not a tour operator. Northern Borealis is an independent guide. We don't run or sell tours ourselves; we point you toward trusted booking platforms and operators, so you can book safely and directly.
