Affiliate Disclosure

How Northern Borealis is funded — and our promise of honest, independent recommendations.

Last updated: July 2026

⚡ In short: Northern Borealis is not a tour operator. We independently review and recommend Northern Lights tours from trusted booking platforms. If you book through our links, we may earn a commission — at no extra cost to you.

Transparency matters to us, so here's the full picture of how Northern Borealis works and how we keep the lights on (pun very much intended).

What Are Affiliate Links?

Northern Borealis participates in affiliate programmes operated by third-party booking platforms and their partners — such as tour marketplaces and travel-booking services. This means that some of the links on our website are "affiliate links."

When you click one of these links and go on to make a booking or purchase, we may receive a small commission from the booking platform. It's a bit like a finder's fee for connecting you with a tour we recommend.

It Never Costs You Anything Extra

This is important: using our links never costs you a penny more. The price you pay is exactly the same as it would be if you visited the booking platform directly. The commission comes out of the platform's margin, not your pocket. In other words, you get the same great tour at the same price — and you help support this site at no cost to yourself.

Our Recommendations Are Independent

Earning a commission does not influence which tours we feature or how we rank them. We recommend and rank tours based on the things that actually matter — real traveller ratings and review counts, group sizes, what's included, guarantees, and overall value.

In short, we recommend the tours we'd happily book for our own friends and family. If a tour isn't worth your money, we won't pretend otherwise — and if we wouldn't book it ourselves, we won't recommend it to you. You can read more about how we work on our About Us page.

We Are Not the Tour Operator

Northern Borealis is an independent guide, not a travel agency or tour operator. We do not run, sell or fulfil any tours directly. All bookings, payments, cancellations, refunds and customer service are handled by the third-party booking platform or the tour operator you book with.

If you have a question about a specific booking — availability, changes, refunds or anything else — please contact the platform or operator you booked through, as they hold all the details of your reservation.

Why Your Support Matters

The commissions we earn are what keep Northern Borealis running, independent and completely free to use. They fund our research, our writing and the trips that let us keep learning about the best aurora experiences in Europe.

Booking through our links is a simple, no-cost way to say thanks — and it helps us keep chasing (and writing about) the Northern Lights for travellers like you. We're genuinely grateful for your support.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how we make our recommendations, we'd be happy to explain. Just head to our contact page and get in touch.
