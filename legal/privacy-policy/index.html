Your Privacy

Privacy Policy

What we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you visit Northern Borealis.

Last updated: July 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Northern Borealis ("we," "us," or "our") collects when you visit our website, how we use it, and the choices and rights you have. By using our site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little personal data as possible. Depending on how you use the site, this may include:

  • • Usage & device data: anonymous or aggregated information such as your browser type, device, operating system, approximate location, pages visited, referring site and time spent — collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • • Cookies & similar technologies: small files stored on your device to help the site function, understand traffic, and track affiliate referrals (see below).
  • • Contact information: only if you voluntarily contact us — in which case we receive your email address and any details you choose to share.

We do not ask for or store sensitive personal information, and we do not process payments on this site — all bookings are handled by third-party platforms.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • • Operate, maintain and improve our website and content.
  • • Understand how visitors find and use the site so we can make it better.
  • • Track referrals to our affiliate partners so bookings can be correctly attributed to us.
  • • Respond to your enquiries when you contact us.
  • • Keep the site secure and detect or prevent technical issues and abuse.

Cookies & Analytics

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. We use them, along with third-party analytics services, to understand site traffic and improve your experience. We may use:

  • • Essential cookies — needed for the site to function correctly.
  • • Analytics cookies — to measure traffic and understand how the site is used (in aggregate).
  • • Affiliate cookies — when you click an affiliate link, our partners may place a cookie to attribute any resulting booking to us.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that if you disable certain cookies, some features of the site may not work as intended.

Affiliate Links & Third Parties

Our site contains links to third-party booking platforms and affiliate partners. When you click through and visit those sites, their own privacy policies and terms apply — not ours. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party websites, and we encourage you to review their policies. You can learn more about how these links work on our Affiliate Disclosure page.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited, non-personal or aggregated data with trusted service providers (such as analytics and hosting providers) purely to operate and improve the site, or where we are legally required to do so.

Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Anonymous analytics data may be retained in aggregate form. If you contact us by email, we keep your message only as long as needed to respond and maintain our records.

Your Rights

Depending on your location (for example, under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, or similar laws), you may have the right to:

  • • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • • Request that we correct or update inaccurate data.
  • • Request that we delete your personal data.
  • • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data.
  • • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via our contact page. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please get in touch via our contact page. We're happy to help.
