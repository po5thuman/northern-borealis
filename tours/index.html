Frequently Asked Questions

🌍 What are the best Northern Lights tours in Europe?
The best Northern Lights tours in Europe depart from Tromsø, Svalbard, Svolvær and Alta in Norway; Abisko and Kiruna in Sweden; Lapland (Rovaniemi and Levi) in Finland; and Reykjavík and Akureyri in Iceland. Each offers a different experience, from guaranteed-sighting chases to luxury cruises and snowmobile adventures.
🏆 Which country is best for Northern Lights tours?
Norway, Sweden, Finland and Iceland are all excellent. Norway (Tromsø) offers the widest choice, Sweden (Abisko) has the clearest skies, Finland (Lapland) combines the aurora with Santa Claus and Christmas magic, and Iceland pairs the Northern Lights with dramatic scenery. The best choice depends on the experience you want.
💶 How much does a Northern Lights tour cost?
Northern Lights tours in Europe typically cost between €57 and €300 per person, depending on the destination, group size and what's included. Budget small-group chases start around €57–€99, while private tours and luxury cruises cost more.
✨ Are Northern Lights tours worth it?
Yes. Local guides track live aurora and weather forecasts and drive you away from city lights and clouds, dramatically improving your chances of a sighting compared to going it alone. Many tours also include photos, warm clothing and refreshments.
